Website Planning Document: Benin Chamber of Commerce

Site Name

The site is named Benin Chamber of Commerce. It represents a professional platform for businesses, entrepreneurs, and investors in Benin City, Edo State.

Site Purpose

The website will serve as the official digital hub for the Chamber, providing business listings, resources, local events, and opportunities for collaboration. It also promotes Edo State’s economic growth and supports small businesses by giving them visibility online.

Scenarios

Visitors might come to the site with the following questions:

  • How can I join the Benin Chamber of Commerce?
  • Where can I find a directory of member businesses?
  • What upcoming events or workshops are available for local entrepreneurs?
  • What resources exist for starting or expanding a business in Edo State?
  • How can I connect with other professionals or investors in Benin?

Color Schema

The site uses colors inspired by Edo culture and professionalism:

  • Primary Color: #8B0000 (Royal Benin Red) – for headers, nav, and accents.
  • Secondary Color: #FFD700 (Gold) – symbolizing prosperity, used for highlights and borders.
  • Neutral Color: #F9F9F9 (Light Gray) – background for readability.
